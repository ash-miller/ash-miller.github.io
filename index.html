Hi, I'm Ash.

I began my creative life as a performance artist, receiving my BFA in Dance from New York University in 2013 where I also actively studied music and created multi-media work. After returning to my hometown of Seattle in 2014 I found myself increasingly drawn to computer graphics and studied 3d at a vocational school in the area. For over a year I have contributed to a small team of artists at Amazon creating models for an array for photo-real 3d experiences. In my spare time I practice yoga, hike, and tend to my indoor and outdoor gardens.
You can learn more about my work in performance at faunix.com
